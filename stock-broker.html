Artificial Intelligence Stock Broker

The A.I. stock broker uses both K nearest neighbor (k-nn) and decision trees in order to learn from previous events. The tensorflow and python based scripts are used to decipher the data. The data is imported from Google Finance APIs in order to get up to date data. News articles are also read through Python in order to look for key factors (oil prices rising or falling for example). The bot is currently in quality assurance testing and learning from its mistakes.
